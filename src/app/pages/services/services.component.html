Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started with WasteZero is easy, but here are some things you might want to know.

We handle recyclable materials (plastics, paper, glass, metals), organic/compostable waste, and even certain hazardous household items like batteries and e-waste. Specific guidelines are available when you schedule a pickup.

Standard recycling pickups for citizens are currently free, supported by our partnerships with sustainable organizations. Premium/commercial bulk pickups may incur a small maintenance fee.

Every volunteer undergoes a brief background check and an orientation session before being allowed to handle pickups or participate in community drives. We prioritize safety and professionalism.
