Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you give us: For example, our services require you to sign up for a WasteZero Account. When you do, we’ll ask for personal information, like your name, email address, telephone number, and location.
  • Information we get from your use of our services: We collect information about the services that you use and how you use them, like when you request a waste pickup or complete a volunteering task.

2. How We Use Information We Collect

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect WasteZero and our users.

We use your email address to send you notifications about your account, such as registration confirmations, security alerts, and updates to our services.

3. Information We Share

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals outside of WasteZero unless one of the following circumstances applies:

  • With your consent: We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of WasteZero when we have your consent to do so.
  • For external processing: We provide personal information to our affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons to process it for us, based on our instructions.
  • For legal reasons: We will share personal information if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary.

4. Information Security

We work hard to protect WasteZero and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

  • We encrypt many of our services using SSL.
  • We review our information collection and storage practices.
  • We restrict access to personal information to designated personnel.

5. Data Retention

We retain the personal information we collect as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to cancel your account, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. Whenever you use our services, we aim to provide you with access to your personal information.
