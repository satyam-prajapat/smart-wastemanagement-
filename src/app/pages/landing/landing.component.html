How WasteZero Works

Our platform connects households and businesses with verified pickup agents and NGOs to ensure responsible waste disposal.

1. Create an Account

Sign up as a User, Pickup Agent, or NGO Admin in just a few clicks to access your tailored dashboard.

2. Schedule a Pickup

Categorize your waste (plastic, organic, e-waste) and select a convenient time for collection.

3. Agent Dispatch

A verified agent in your area is intelligently assigned to collect your categorized recyclables.
